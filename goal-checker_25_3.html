📖 Instruction Manual

A quick guide to how Quest Log works — for new adventurers and reviewers alike.

🛡️ The Scroll of Purpose

Quest Log is a gamified productivity sanctuary designed to transform your daily tasks into heroic quests and your long-term ambitions into epic achievements. By blending RPG progression mechanics with psychological motivation principles, Quest Log helps you conquer procrastination, maintain consistent daily streaks, and invest your hard-earned gold in custom, real-life rewards.

🏠 Dashboard

Your home base. It shows your current Level, Rank title, EXP bar, day streak, and any active blessing. Below that you'll find your active quests and long-term quests, ready to be completed.

➕ Add Quest

Create a one-off task ("quest") or a long-term quest with multiple check-ins. Each quest has an Intensity (Low, Medium, High, Very High) which determines how much EXP it's worth — harder tasks pay out more. You can also add optional tags and a deadline.

📜 Quest Log

A full history of every quest you've completed, with filters by tag, intensity, or date — handy for reviewing what you've gotten done.

🏆 Levels & EXP

Completing quests earns EXP based on their intensity — every quest of the same intensity earns exactly the same EXP while you're on the same level. Fill the EXP bar to level up; each new level raises how much EXP that intensity is worth, but level-ups get a little harder to reach the further you go. Your Rank title upgrades as you climb.

🔥 Streaks

Completing at least one quest a day builds your streak. Miss a day and it resets (unless you've got a Streak Freeze) — consistency is rewarded with achievements and bragging rights.

✨ Blessings

Two gods can bless you, each for a different reason:

  • 🍈 Melon — deity of consistency. Blessed by leveling up, or by finishing a long-term quest normally. Trait: grants 2 bonus ❄️ Streak Freezes and fully shields your streak from resetting while active.
  • 🚁 Helikopter — deity of speed. Blessed by finishing a long-term quest well ahead of its deadline. Trait: 3x EXP on High and Very High intensity quests while active — a good time to tackle your hardest tasks.

Each blessing lasts a limited number of hours, and each god's favor is tracked separately in Stats.

🛒 Shop

You earn Gold whenever you level up. Spend it in the Shop, where you can create your own custom rewards (like "watch an episode") with a Gold cost, then redeem them once you've saved up enough.

🏆 Stats

Track your Achievements — badges unlocked for milestones like streaks and totals — your Deity Favor with Melon and Helikopter, plus a full completed-quest log.

🎨 Themes

Your Sanctuary's look upgrades as you level up. Dark mode is available from the start, and each new premium theme — like Violet Oasis or Gold Vault — unlocks automatically the moment your Level reaches its requirement, no purchase needed. Open ⚙ Settings → Themes Selection to see every theme, including ones you haven't unlocked yet (shown locked with the Level required). Tap any unlocked theme to apply it instantly.

At Level 10+, a Custom Theme color picker unlocks in that same panel. Instead of choosing a preset, you can pick any background color you like — just tap the color swatch, choose your color, and your Sanctuary re-themes around it immediately. Your choice (preset or custom) is saved automatically and stays applied next time you open the app.

☁️ Cloud Sync

Cloud Sync lets your progress follow you to another device using a simple sync code you choose yourself — no account or password needed. Find it under ⚙ Settings → Cloud Sync & Data Management.

  • Connect & Save — backs up your current progress to the cloud under your sync code. Once connected, every future save on this device quietly re-backs-up to the cloud too, so you never have to remember to do it again.
  • Load on this Device — enter the same sync code on another device (or a fresh browser) and this pulls your most recent cloud backup down, replacing whatever is currently on that device with it.

In plain terms: saving pushes your local progress up to the cloud; loading pulls it back down and overwrites local data with it. Because loading overwrites, only load onto a device you're okay resetting to your last cloud backup — and your local save on this device always keeps working exactly as before, cloud sync or not.

💡 Tip: your ⚙ Settings menu lets you customize how much EXP each intensity is worth, and export or import your data.
